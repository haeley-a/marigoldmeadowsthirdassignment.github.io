Marigold Meadows - About Us

Our Story: At Marigold Meadows, we believe in the transformative power of flowers. Established in 2002 by floral enthusiasts Emma and William Greenfield, Marigold Meadows Floral Company has blossomed from a small local shop into a cherished destination for floral artistry. Inspired by the vibrant hues and delicate fragrances of nature, our journey began with a simple mission: to spread joy and beauty through the language of flowers.

Meet Our Team

Emma Greenfield - Co-founder & Creative Director:

Emma's passion for floristry stems from her deep connection to nature and her innate creativity. With over two decades of experience, she leads our team with a keen eye for design and a commitment to excellence.

William Greenfield - Co-founder & Operations Manager:

William's meticulous attention to detail ensures the seamless operation of Marigold Meadows. From sourcing the finest blooms to managing logistics, he plays a pivotal role in bringing our floral visions to life.

Sophie Martinez - Lead Floral Designer:

Sophie's artistic flair and expertise in floral design elevate every arrangement she creates. With a background in fine arts and a passion for botanicals, she infuses each creation with elegance and sophistication.

Michael Thompson - Customer Experience Specialist:

Michael's warm demeanor and dedication to customer satisfaction make him an invaluable member of our team. Whether assisting clients in-store or over the phone, he ensures that every interaction with Marigold Meadows is memorable.

Our Mission

At Marigold Meadows, we are committed to crafting exquisite floral arrangements that inspire joy, celebrate life's moments, and forge meaningful connections. Guided by a reverence for nature and a dedication to craftsmanship, we strive to exceed our customers' expectations with every bouquet we create. With each petal meticulously placed, we seek to spread love, beauty, and happiness, one bloom at a time.
